Interesting Pickleball Facts

Home Picture Album
  1. Pickleball was invented in 1965.
  2. Pickleball was named after one of the inventors, Congressman Joel Pritchard's dog, whose name was Pickles.
  3. Unlike tennis or ping-pong balls, pickleballs have holes.
  • In pickleball, players are referred to as "Picklers", and those who have lost did not "lose", they have "Pickled".
  • USAPA recently estimated that over 400,000 people actively play pickleball on a regular basis.
  • Due to a pickleballs large holes, they travel approximately 1/3 the speed of a tennis ball.
